About Me




Xochitl Mora is a web developer and currently enrolled in MIT xPRO’s Professional Certificate in Coding Program. She was introduced to the magical world of coding by her brother, a back end developer.

For the last 12 years, Xochitl worked in agriculture by owning and operating a small organic farm. As an entrepreneur she oversaw every aspect of the business and wore all the hats: from production management, marketing, sales, customer service and accounting.

Xochitl currently lives on the North Shore of Maui, Hawaii. When she isn’t writing code you can find her surfing her local breaks, cooking, and pursuing new hobbies that challenge her mind.

Web Development

My expertise in web development includes programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.JS, as well as proficiency with frameworks and libraries like Bootstrap, Jest, Google Maps API, and Google Fonts. I'm also skilled in using tools and software such as Git, VS Code, and Agile methodologies. With a particular interest in API's and CSS, I'm passionate about creating dynamic and engaging user experiences through intuitive interfaces and clean, efficient code.
